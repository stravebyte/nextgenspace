Sagittarius A

The supermassive black hole at the center of our galaxy, the Milky Way. Its mass is about 4 million times that of the Sun.

Dark Matter and Dark Energy

These mysterious substances make up about 27% and 68% of the universe, respectively. Dark matter does not emit light or energy, making it invisible, while dark energy is thought to be responsible for the accelerated expansion of the universe.
